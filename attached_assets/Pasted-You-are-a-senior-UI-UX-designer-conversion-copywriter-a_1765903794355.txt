You are a senior UI/UX designer, conversion copywriter and web developer.

Your task is to generate a high-conversion, professional one-page website for a freelance Web Developer who sells websites and online presence to businesses, professionals and entrepreneurs.

The main goal of this website is to build trust, authority and convert visitors into qualified leads via WhatsApp or contact button.

The website must feel strategic, premium and focused on business results, not just visual design.

⸻

=================================

GENERAL GUIDELINES

=================================
	•	Website language: Portuguese (Brazil)
	•	Tone: professional, clear, confident and persuasive (never aggressive)
	•	Target audience: small businesses, professionals and entrepreneurs who want to look more credible and professional online
	•	Focus on benefits, clarity and real results, avoiding technical jargon
	•	Copy must be simple, direct, scannable and conversion-focused
	•	Avoid long paragraphs
	•	Use short sentences and bullet points whenever possible

⸻

=================================

POSITIONING & DIFFERENTIATION

=================================
	•	Position the developer as a strategic partner, not just someone who “creates websites”
	•	Emphasize:
	•	Focus on credibility, conversion and online presence
	•	Custom projects, no generic templates
	•	Clear communication and structured process
	•	Use soft authority (no fake testimonials or exaggerated claims)
	•	Avoid buzzwords and empty promises

⸻

=================================

VISUAL STYLE & DESIGN

=================================
	•	Modern, clean and premium look
	•	Minimalist layout with strong visual hierarchy
	•	Plenty of white space
	•	Neutral color palette (white, dark gray and a soft accent color)
	•	Modern sans-serif typography
	•	Subtle animations and smooth transitions
	•	Mobile-first design
	•	Fully responsive (mobile, tablet and desktop)
	•	Professional, trustworthy and timeless aesthetic

⸻

=================================

PAGE STRUCTURE & CONTENT

=================================

1. HERO SECTION
	•	Strong and clear headline about professional websites that build trust and generate clients
	•	Short subheadline explaining the value proposition in a simple way
	•	Two clear CTAs:
* “Solicitar orçamento”
* “Falar no WhatsApp”
	•	Include subtle microcopy near CTAs to reduce friction
(ex: “Resposta rápida”, “Sem compromisso”)

⸻

2. PROBLEM SECTION
	•	Headline addressing the lack of online presence and credibility
	•	Short explanation about how businesses lose opportunities without a professional website
	•	Bullet points with common pains:
* No professional online presence
* Outdated, slow or amateur websites
* Total dependence on Instagram or social media
* Low trust and low conversion

⸻

3. SOLUTION SECTION
	•	Headline explaining that the developer builds online presence the right way
	•	Short paragraph reinforcing strategy, clarity and results
	•	Feature cards:
* Professional websites and landing pages
* Fast performance and optimized loading
* Responsive and mobile-first design
* Conversion-focused structure
* Secure and stable setup

⸻

4. AUTHORITY & TRUST SECTION

Headline: “Por que escolher meu trabalho?”
	•	Bullet points highlighting:
* Focus on real business results
* Custom projects, no ready-made templates
* Clear process and direct communication
* Strategic thinking beyond visual design

⸻

5. PROCESS SECTION

Simple and visual step-by-step timeline:
	1.	Understanding the business and goals
	2.	Structure and design planning
	3.	Website development
	4.	Review and adjustments
	5.	Final delivery with initial support

⸻

6. SERVICES SECTION

Service cards:
	•	Site institucional
	•	Landing page de vendas
	•	Página profissional
	•	Manutenção e melhorias de site
	•	Integrações com WhatsApp, formulários e ferramentas

Keep descriptions short, benefit-oriented and easy to understand.

⸻

7. FINAL CALL TO ACTION
	•	Strong, confident headline inviting action
	•	Short supporting text reinforcing trust and clarity
	•	Primary CTA button:
“Falar comigo agora”
	•	Include microcopy for reassurance
(ex: “Atendimento direto”, “Sem obrigação”)

⸻

8. FOOTER
	•	Minimal and clean footer
	•	Developer name or brand
	•	Role: Web Developer
	•	Contact links (WhatsApp, email or social)
	•	Copyright text

⸻

=================================

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

=================================
	•	Fast loading performance
	•	SEO-friendly structure
	•	Semantic HTML structure
	•	Accessible design
	•	Clear and visible CTAs throughout the page

⸻

=================================

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS

=================================
	•	Deliver a ready-to-use one-page website structure
	•	All content written in Portuguese (Brazil)
	•	Clear visual section separation
	•	Focused on conversion, trust and authority
	•	Content only (no CSS), structured in a way that can easily be implemented
